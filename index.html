Focused on the Netherlands

We specialise in the Dutch higher education system, guiding students who want to study in the Netherlands — one of Europe’s most international and innovative academic environments.

We work especially with universities in cities such as Amsterdam, Rotterdam, Utrecht, Groningen, Eindhoven and Leiden.

Main universities we advise on include:
University of Amsterdam (UvA), Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam (VU), Utrecht University, Leiden University, Erasmus University Rotterdam, TU Delft, Eindhoven University of Technology and University of Groningen.
